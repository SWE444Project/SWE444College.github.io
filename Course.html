Computer Engneering

About Course COE200

Introduction to Computer Engineering. Digital Circuits. Boolean algebra and
switching theory. Manipulation and minimization of Boolean functions.
Combinational circuits analysis and design, multiplexers, decoders and adders.
Sequential circuit analysis and design, basic flip-flops, clocking and edgetriggering,
registers, counters, timing sequences, state assignment and reduction
techniques. Register transfer level operations.
